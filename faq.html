Frequently Asked Questions

Getting to TAEHS!

We will be at Thomas A. Edison CTE HS's Cafeteria and Library at 165-65 84th Avenue, Jamaica, New York 11432. Enter through the main entrance. Jamaica, Queens is accessible via the E, J, Z, and F train within a 30 minute walk of the station.

Time

Doors will open at 8AM and the event will end at 8PM. Arrive from 8-9 AM.

What to Bring

Please bring your laptops and chargers. This is a day event so pack lightly.

Who's eligible to come

All High School students will be welcome to participate and compete. Those not yet in high school can contact us for more information.

Cost to Join

This is a free event.

Food

We will provide food, snacks, and drinks.

Prizes

Prizes will be announced during the hackathon.

How to Help?

Help spread the word about our first event. It would help us have a diverse group of people joining, along with gaining connections with potential sponsors.

Donating

Donations help us improve the quality of opportunities for our members. We are a fiscally sponsored organization and donations are tax deductible. Link for Donations

Why should you come

Hackathons are a great opportunity to meet new people, learn new skills, and gain access to opportunities that you wouldn't have access to in the future. If you're in doubt, feel free to contact us.
